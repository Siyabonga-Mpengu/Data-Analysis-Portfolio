Financial Model
in Excel (Nedbank Covid Payment Holiday)

This project, posed by Nedbank, was part of the QYF Virtual Internship. The task was to develop a model to choose, from a dataset of clients, which clients would receive a payment holiday on their loans. I chose clients who had debt-income ratio greater than 0.36 credit ratio of less than 705 and had never defaulted on their loans. With this model default costs went from R5 949 612,50 on the old process to R883 600 - a 85% reduction.
